A guide to using this webpage

About this page

This is a "game-ified" resume/portfolio page. It contains my resume, some of my photography work, and some other information about me. It is intended to be a unique way to get to know me and showcase my abilities.

Controls

WASD or Arrow Keys to move the avatar
Bright blue boxes represent accessible pages
Press space while the avatar is within a blue box to open the page
Press the jumping back button or [esc] to return to the main page
Many pages are scrollable

Game Controls

Move near a star icon to collect it!
You can collect 5 stars at a time!
Enter the Deposit Stars box and press space to deposit them
Once you have collected 4 stars, a new color avatar will unlock!
As you collect more stars, more avatars will unlock. You change your avatar by pressing the 1, 2, 3, or 4 keys.
There are a total of 16 stars to collect!
Hold shift to sprint
If the page is refreshed, the game progress will reset

Portfolio Controls

Scrolling with the cursor outside of a image section scrolls the page sideways
To scroll within a section, hover over the section, then scroll as normally
To return to side scrolling, hover outside any image sections
Use the navbar at the top to more easily navigate sections
