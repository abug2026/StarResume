About this page

This is a "game-ified" resume/portfolio page. It contains my resume, some of my photography work, and some other information about me. It is intended to be a unique way to get to know me and showcase my abilities.

Resume Controls

WASD or Arrow Keys to move the avatar
Bright blue boxes represent accessible pages
Press space while the avatar is within a blue box to open the page
Press the jumping back button to return to the main page
Hold shift to sprint

Game controls

Move near a star icon to collect it!
You can collect 5 stars at a time!
Enter the depositStars box and press space to deposit them
Once you have collected 4 stars, a new color avatar will unlock!
As you collect more stars, more avatars will unlock. You can return to a prior avatar by pressing the 1, 2, 3, or 4 keys.
